Relationships

One of the most important thing in life is of course love and if that is not working this manifests on all other aspects. There are plenty serious problems that can happen in relationship: cheating a partner, abusive behavior towards partner, disrespect etc. These and many other problems are all serious and sometimes you will need 3rd person to help you to solve your problems.

Be realistic!

If you already notice the problem which is hurting you and that behavior is repeating than you must leave that person immediately! You can not deny the problem, that way it won't dissapear and you are making your life more and more miserable.

Abusive relationship

Abusive relationships come in all forms along with physical abuse — social abuse, emotional abuse, sexual abuse (we are not referring here to molestation), financial abuse, etc.

Bad communication

Communication is actually key to everything, especially to successful relationship. If you believe something is going on with your partner you should better explain immediately, and not be trigged in cycle from which you can not find escape.

Cheating

This is one of the most serious and ending points of every relationship, firstly and most importantly you owe to tell your partner everything if you are a cheater, then you should talk and try if it is possible to find a solution which will hurt the least.

Signs of a bad relationship

How to notice and recognize poisonous relationship?

  • If you are afraid of your partner
  • If you believe that you deserve to be hurt or mistreated
  • If you feel emotionally numb or helpless
  • If you feel insecure
  • If your partner humiliate or yell at you
  • If your partner criticize you and put you down
  • If your partner destroy your belongings

Important!

  • Don't overreact if someone is behaving like this it must not mean that you should end a relationship immediately, everyone can sometimes be nervous, but if it's repeating RUN!

Ways to improve relationship:

Talk Conversation in most cases can solve everything
Search for a help If you are being abused by your partner,leave the partner and even talk to authorities if you are afraid for your life
Consult Sometimes a consulting therapist or a psychologist can help a lot, so you could always try this for instance to save marriage
Love and be loved In most cases you receive what you give, so before you accuse someone of being bad partner think of your behavior and affections.
